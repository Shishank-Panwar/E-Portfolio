Lab 2: Assignments & E-Portfolio

A briefing on semester assignments and final portfolio submission.

Summary

This session focused on the semester's assignments and the final e-portfolio submission. The instructor explained the importance of an online portfolio and how to organize work for the final submission in October.

Assignment Details

  • Assignment 1: Create a professional LinkedIn profile, complete with a headline, summary, and experience sections.
  • Assignment 2: Record and submit a self-introduction video based on the preparation checklist from Lab 1.

E-Portfolio Requirements

Students are required to design and host an e-portfolio website to showcase all lab work. It must include links to the LinkedIn profile and the self-introduction video, with a final submission deadline in October.
