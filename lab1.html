Lab 1: Career Skills & PD

An introduction to soft skills and personality development.

Summary

The first session introduced the subject *Career Skills & Personality Development — III*, walking us through the syllabus and learning outcomes. It focused on improving self-introduction, communication, public speaking, and teamwork.

Topics Covered

  • Self Introduction – II: Preparation and video presentation.
  • Art of Conversation – II: Speeches and small talk practice.
  • Concept of OB: Motivation, team-building, and leadership basics.
  • Public Speaking – II: Extempore, JAM sessions, and story-building.
  • Interview Skills: Mock interviews and preparation checklists.

In-Class Activity

Each student gave a short self-introduction to practice Unit 1 skills. This activity provided quick feedback and helped identify areas for improvement before the formal video recording.

Learning Outcomes

  • Build a concise and confident self-introduction.
  • Understand basic public speaking techniques.
  • Recognise components of teamwork and leadership.
  • Prepare for mock interviews and common criteria.
